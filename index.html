The Asikhanyise Foundation is a Non-Profit Organization with a difference, 'Asikhanyise' directly translated means, 'Let us light the way' and that is what we plan to do. Our logo represents what we stand for which is to be the light in our community and the City. We believe that we have one planet and it is our job to protect and preserve it, we take pride in our beautiful city Durban and its beautiful beaches that is why we curate monthly beach clean ups with the assistance of our municipality and community. All plastic waste collected is processed by us an our local arts and crafts team that turns waste into beautiful furniture and jewellery. Every summer to raise funds for young adults the Asikhanyise Foundation curates a Beach Festival called 'Beachday DBN', a ticketed event of live music, food stalls and fun in the sun. Asikhanyise Foundation is for the Youth by the Youth!
